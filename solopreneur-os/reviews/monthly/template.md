## Rules

1. **Review weekly summaries first**. Don't re-analyze raw data.
2. **Numbers before narratives**. Look at data, then tell stories.
3. **One sitting**. Schedule 2 hours, finish in one block.
4. **Decisions required**. Every section ends with an action or conclusion.
