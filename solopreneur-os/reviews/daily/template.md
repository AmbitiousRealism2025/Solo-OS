## Rules

1. **First instinct answers**. Don't overthink.
2. **Skip days, don't backfill**. Start fresh each day.
3. **Under 5 minutes**. If it takes longer, you're writing too much.
4. **Honest > Positive**. Track what happened, not what you wanted to happen.
