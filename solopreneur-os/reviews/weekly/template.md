## Rules

1. **Use daily captures only**. If you need to dig for data, your daily check-ins are too sparse.
2. **Complete in one sitting**. Don't stretch this across multiple days.
3. **Honest > Positive**. Bad weeks have lessons. Whitewashing hides them.
4. **End with decisions**. Not feelings.
