## Shipping Checklist

Before publishing:

- [ ] Does this serve my audience's actual problem?
- [ ] Is this something I'd want to read/watch?
- [ ] Does it reflect my actual expertise (not AI slop)?
- [ ] Is there a clear takeaway?
- [ ] Does it connect to my offers/services (if appropriate)?
