## Project Quality Signals

### Red Flags (Watch For)

- [ ] Scope creeping without rate adjustment
- [ ] Delayed payments
- [ ] Excessive revision rounds
- [ ] Energy drain during/after calls
- [ ] Dreading the work

### Green Flags (Seek More)

- [ ] Work energizes you
- [ ] Client values your expertise
- [ ] Clear scope and boundaries
- [ ] On-time payments
- [ ] Would refer to others
