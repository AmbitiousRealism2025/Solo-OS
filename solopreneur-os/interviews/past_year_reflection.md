# Past Year Reflection Interview

> Self-interview for annual narrative construction. **Timebox: 45-60 minutes.**

---

## Purpose

Extract the story of your year. Not a list of accomplishments—a narrative of what happened, what it meant, and what you learned.

---

## Instructions

- Answer in writing, not just in your head
- First instinct answers. Don't overthink.
- Use "WHAT" questions, not "WHY" (what happened, not why you feel a certain way)
- Complete this BEFORE the [Annual Review Framework](../frameworks/annual_review.md)
