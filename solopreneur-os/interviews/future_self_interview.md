# Future Self Interview

> Self-interview for 3-year vision construction. **Timebox: 30-45 minutes.**

---

## Purpose

Interview your future self to extract a concrete, achievable vision. This feeds into [Vivid Vision](../frameworks/vivid_vision.md) and goal-setting.

---

## Instructions

- Write in present tense as if you ARE your future self
- Be specific, not aspirational-vague
- Include constraints and trade-offs, not just wins
- Complete this BEFORE writing your Vivid Vision

---
